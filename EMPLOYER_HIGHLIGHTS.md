   - Hierarchical clustering approach

   - Multiple metrics (degree, betweenness, eigenvector)
   - Strategic node ranking
   - Bridge node identification

3. **Threshold Activation Model**
   - Stochastic information propagation
   - Configurable adoption thresholds
   - Iterative cascade simulation
